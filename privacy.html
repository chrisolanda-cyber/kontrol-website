Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Kontrol AI ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you interact with us, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to our website and to enquiries you make with us.

01 Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to give us. When you submit an enquiry through our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name
  • Your phone number
  • Any additional details you include in your message

We do not collect sensitive personal information, and we do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising trackers on this website.

02 How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and arrange a discovery call
  • Understand your workflow and scope potential automation work
  • Communicate with you about services you have requested

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

03 How We Store & Protect It

Enquiry submissions are transmitted securely and delivered to us by email through our form provider. We retain your information only for as long as needed to respond to your enquiry and to maintain a record of our correspondence. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.

04 Third Parties

We use a third-party form service to process and deliver contact form submissions to us by email. This provider processes your data only to deliver your message and does not use it for its own marketing. We do not share your information with any other third parties except where required by law.

05 Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to ask us to correct it, or to request that we delete it. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

06 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
